Hearty Viking Stew

A staple in Viking diet.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Beef or Venison, Cubed
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 3 carrots, chopped
  • 2 parnips, chopped
  • 2 turnips, chopped
  • 4 cups Beef Broth
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Ground Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp Dried Thyme
  • 1 tsp Driedd Sage
  • 1 tsp Dried Rosemary
